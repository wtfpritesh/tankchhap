№ 04
Heritage

Seventy-six monsoons and counting.

1949
The year we began

A craft passed down across three generations.

Ratlam, 1949. A small workshop. A simple promise: when the sky opens, the umbrella holds. Three quarters of a century later, the workshop has grown, the catalogue has widened, the family has built three distinct brands, and the doors are open in Indore and Mumbai — but the promise is the same one we started with.

We make rain essentials. We make them well. We make them in India, for India.
